| ---------------------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Model block                  | `<module>`        | `<module>.<model>`        | `<block variant>` | Example: `blog.article` + `card`.                      |

## Metadata Contract

- `block.manifest.json` / `page.manifest.json` and the paired `figma.json` must agree on `componentName`, `pageName`, `variantName`, `nodeId`, and `variantNodeId`.
- `figma.syncStatus` uses only `not-created`, `created`, `needs-update`, or `verified`; legacy `pending` and `synced` are invalid.
- `figma.json.metadata` is the local mirror for Figma shared plugin data keys under `sps.drafts.*`, `sps.figma.*`, `sps.source.*`, and `sps.code.*`.
- Storybook screenshot references are named review nodes beside added/updated components. They are not component sets, variants, or managed SPS implementation nodes.
